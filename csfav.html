My Favorite Languages and Tools!

Java is one of my favorite programming languages. It's the first one I've learned, and the one I've used the most in my projects by far. In particular I like creating GUI applications with Swing and JavaFX, which I've done both in Eclipse and NetBeans.

Python, is another favorite of mine, as it's relatively simple and fast to get a program made. In particular I like it for making simple scripts and also games with the Pygame library.

Godot! Not a language (although it does have it's own built-in scripting langauge called GDScript), but my preferred game engine, mainly due to it's pricing and licensing, but it's also an elegant enough engine especially for one thats completely free and open-source.
